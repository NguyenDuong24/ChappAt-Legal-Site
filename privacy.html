Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

At ChappAt, your privacy is our top priority. This policy explains how we protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect basic information when you register such as Name, Email, phone number, and profile information (photo, age, bio). If you enable Radar, we will collect your GPS location data.

2. How We Use Data

Your data is used to:

  • Connect you with surrounding users.
  • Provide "Crossing Paths" feature.
  • Improve user experience and system security.

3. Data Security

We use standard encryption measures and store data on the secure Firebase platform. We strictly do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, edit, or request the deletion of your personal data at any time through the settings in the application.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about the privacy policy, please contact us via email: support@chappat.com
